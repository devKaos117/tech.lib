## Penetration Testing
A penetration test is an authorized simulated cyberattack on a computer system, performed to evaluate it's security and attempt to [[Exploit|exploit]] identified [[Vulnerability|vulnerabilities]] to determine the actual business [[Security Assessments#Risk|risk]] and the potential for a breach.

### Modalities
- **Black Box:** The tester has no prior knowledge of the target system, simulating a realistic external attack
- **Gray Box:** The tester has partial knowledge of the system, simulating a compromised user or insider threat
- **White Box:** The tester has full access to source code, architecture, and configuration, effectively working as an audit
