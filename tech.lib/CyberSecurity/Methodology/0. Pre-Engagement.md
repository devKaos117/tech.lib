## Definition
Phase in which the details of the test are defined, where documents, goals and many aspects are agreed upon, such as:
- **Contract:** Initial contract establishing the general service, payment and rules
- **[NDA]{Non-Disclosure Agreement}:** Secrecy contract between the client and the contractor regarding all written or verbal information concerning the project
- **Scope:** A list of individual components to be tested
- **Time Estimation:** Definition of a precise time window to perform the test, and the duration of the time windows for each phase of the attack
- **Limitations and Rules of Engagement:** Specific limitations on machines or services prohibited of exploitation
